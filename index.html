Features of this site:

Complete Annotated List

All 132 species of Odonates (88 dragonflies and 44 damselflies) that have occurred on the Delmarva, including status, habitat and updated flight dates for all species (compiled from personal notes and from Beaton 2007, Barlow et al 2009, White 2011 and NJ Odes Website). Resources Page

Galleries of high resolution, full screen photos

Click on photos to be taken to full galleries with many full screen photos of different behaviors, color types, sexes and ages. Close-ups of critical field marks included for some species. 120 species illustrated (77 dragonflies and 43 damselflies) .

Site Guide

Guide to the best places to find Odonates in the Delmarva.

Natually posed and lighted photos

All photos are naturally posed and naturally lighted, unless noted otherwise. All have been extensively processed in Photoshop using Topaz filters.

Tips for using this site:

Viewing Photos and Galleries

All species accounts contain representative photos of males and females, if available. Click on a photo to be taken to the complete photo gallery for that species.

Dates

In the galleries, click on the information icon to see when the photo was taken.

Locations

All photos were taken in Delaware, unless stated otherwise. All locations are indicated in the photo caption (all are easily found by searching Google Maps).
